Star War

After Disney's acquisition of Lucasfilm, only the later one remained canon. Three live-action Star Wars series will be released on Disney+. The first, The Mandalorian, premiered on November 12, 2019. Star Wars won the 2020 Webby Award for Television & Film in the category Social. Let's see the characters in Star Wars through the art of Origami.

1. Resistance X Wing

click to watch image source

Designed by Karol Kafarski - Folded by Michael Novio. Took about 3 hours or so to fold. Paper: 35cm square of Kami.

2. AT-AT

click to watch image source

Designed - Folded by Karol Kafarski All Terrain Armored Transport (AT-AT). All Terrain Armored Transport. Star Wars vehicle.

3. BB-8

click to watch image source

Designed - Folded by Martin Hunt One sheet of 10" paper back foil cut into quarters lengthwise and widthwise to make 16 equal squares, folded to form 4 different types of module. Locking folds have been improved for neatness and security and ease of assembly, and head proportions modifed to sit more snugly on the ball.
