Flower Origami

Our life is full of colors and so are the flowers. Flowers are an Endless theme and an endless source of inspiration for Origami artisans, There are two main schools of flower folding. The other is to show the angle for flowers such as copper cacti or flowers related to hard stem plants. The second Style is to show softness that focuses on the leaf and petal designs. Let's take a look at some beautiful designs that we have collected.

1. Roses

click to watch image source

Designed - Folded by Peter Keller The first flower that comes to mind and the most popular is the rose. Rose is a very difficult flower in Origami because you have to make each petal and small parts in the pistil. This is one of the best rose designs we could look for. Paper: 156 x 156 cm pelure.
